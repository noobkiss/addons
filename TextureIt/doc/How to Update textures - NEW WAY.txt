How To extract and update the textures list with a new ESO release: (the @Masteroshi430 way)

needed :
Black book browser 4 https://www.esoui.com/downloads/info2532-BlackBookBrowser.html
Notepad++ https://notepad-plus-plus.org/downloads/

**** LET'S GO ****

Black book browser:
    double click on depot\eso.mnf
	on the right panel click on "Save TextureList"
	double click on game\client\game.mnf
	on the right panel click on "Save TextureList"

Notepad++:

    copy the entries in game_texturelist.txt at the end of eso_texturelist.txt
	delete everything above the 1st art/fx entry (otherwise there will be too much data) (SHIFT+CRTL+HOME to delete all above your current line)
	also remove every path not starting by /art or /esoui or /resources
	replace (search > replace) all .dds by .dds',
	(regular expression search mode) replace (search > replace) all ^ by [1]=' 

	
	(in case ZOS did dds.dds files) search for .dds',.dds', and replace it to .dds'
	
    *let's increment the table index numbers*
	Place your text cursor to the top left corner of the 1 column.
	In scroll bar on right side, drag the thumb to scroll to other end of the file.
	Alt + Shift + Left-click into the bottom right corner of the 1 column. (that selects the entire column of 1)
	Edit > column editor  
	select Number to insert
	initial number: 1 
	Increase by: 1
	press ok

	insert the begining and the end of Textureit/core/TextureIt_TexturePaths.lua (it's faster to do it that way)
	
rename your eso_texturelist.txt to TextureIt_TexturePaths.lua and replace it in the addon folder (in the Textureit/core/ folder)
