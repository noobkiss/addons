** Changing Fonts **

1. Download your desired font in TTF format.
2. Copy & Paste into the FontChanger/Fonts folder.
3. Rename your new font to match the font you want to replace 
(and delete the old font). 
4. That's it!

** Important! All Filetypes must be .ttf format **

** Notes **

The 'RegularFont' and 'RegularFont_Bold' fonts are the ones most widely 
used in the UI (Items, Tooltips, Menus etc).

The 'ChatFont' is the font used in the Chat Box.

The 'ScriptureFont' is used for Books/Lore Items and similar.
